Who Are We ?

Connectomedia is India's first "Trust-First" Influencer Marketplace. We are not just another directory of influencers; we are a secure ecosystem designed to protect both Brands and Creators.

The idea was born at IIIT Una, where we realized a massive problem: The influencer market is the "Wild West." Brands fear fake followers, and Creators fear payment delays. We decided to fix this.

Our Mission

We exist to make influencer marketing safe, simple, and standardized.

How Are We Different ?

Five pillars that make Connectomedia the most trusted platform

100% Payment Protection (Escrow)

We don't just introduce you; we protect your money.

  • Brands pay upfront into our secure Escrow Vault
  • Funds locked until work is submitted and approved
  • Zero scams, zero delays

The "Two-Way" Rating System

On most apps, accountability is one-sided. At Connectomedia, we believe in mutual respect.

  • Creators also rate Brands on payment speed and clarity
  • Filters out unprofessional behavior from both sides

Smart-Brief Contracts

Misunderstandings kill campaigns. We replace vague DMs with our Smart Brief Builder.

  • Deliverables, revisions, and timelines locked in digital contract
  • You get exactly what you asked for

Clear Content Licensing

No more legal gray areas. Brands choose upfront how they will use the content.

  • Options: Organic, Paid Ads, or Full Rights
  • Creators get paid fairly for usage
  • Brands stay legally safe

Fair Dispute Resolution

What if something goes wrong? We don't leave you hanging.

  • Human-Mediated Dispute Resolution
  • Our team reviews the Smart Brief and evidence
  • Resolve conflicts fairly and release funds to the right party
